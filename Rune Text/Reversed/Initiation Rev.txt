A counsel against expecting too much, or expecting in the ordinary way, for the old way has come to and end; you simply cannot repeat the old and not suffer.  Reversed, this Rune puts you on notice to call in your scattered energies and concentrate on you own life at this moment, you own requirements for growth.  More important, this Rune counsels you not to focus on outcomes, or to bind yourself with the memory of past achievements; in so doing, you rob yourself of a true present, which is the only time in which self-change can be realized.

You may fee overwhelmed with exhaustion from meeting obstruction upon obstruction in your passage.  Yet you always have a choice, you can see all this apparent negativity as "bad luck" or you can recognize it as an obstacle course, a challenge peculiar to the initiation you are presently undergoing.  Then each setback, each humiliation becomes a test of character.  When your inner being is shifting and reforming on a deep level, patience , constancy and perseverance are called for.  So stay centered, see the humor, and keep on keeping on.

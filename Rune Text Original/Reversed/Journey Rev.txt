Receiving this Rune Reversed puts you on notice to be particularly attentive to personal relationships.  At this time, ruptures are more likely that reconciliations.  Effort will be required on your part.  Keep your good humor; whatever happens, how you respond is up to you.

The requirements of your process may totally disrupt what you had intended.  Hoped-for outcomes may elude you.  And yet what you regard as detours, inconveniences, disruptions, blockages and even failure and deaths will actually be rerouting opportunities, with union and reunion as the only abiding destinations.
